Name of the Domain:

Health and Wellness Management

The Problem:

Many people struggle to understand complex health information such as medical prescriptions, long health articles, wellness guides, and doctor instructions. This difficulty often leads to confusion, poor decision-making, and lack of clarity in managing personal health. Users need a simple and reliable way to interpret health information without stress.

Target Users:

Individuals seeking clearer understanding of health-related content

Patients needing simplified explanations of medical notes or instructions

Students and adults who want quick summaries of health articles

Anyone overwhelmed by lengthy or technical health information

How Your Solution Addresses It:

The Health & Wellness web application provides an intelligent and easy-to-use platform that simplifies complex health content using AI.

Key Features:

AI-Powered Health Text Summarizer:
Converts long health documents into short, easy-to-understand summaries.

User-Friendly Interface:
Clean, accessible design suitable for all age groups.

Improved Health Awareness:
Helps users understand prescriptions, health guides, and wellness articles clearly.

Time-Saving Tool:
Provides fast, accurate summaries to support better health decisions.

This project empowers individuals to manage their health knowledge more confidently and efficiently.
